A little description of Awero
Awero is a damsel, a Queen that took beauty from where it was housed, Awelewa as in yoruba decent, meaning the beautiful one. She is Gorgeous, Smart, Intelligent. She is a support anyone could ever dreamt of. A genius and Iron Lady, Forever will you be remembered for all you do You are the best. So short of words, on this sixth moon of the amazing journey. You were always there and you still are, you have made me think of possibilities in times of challenges. Am always happy and comfortable with you and when am around you I find peace.
